Adding price data to CALC

Now contracting officers and other acquisition professionals can add price lists directly to CALC. And it only takes about a minute.

{% if user.is_authenticated %} Add price lists {% else %} Log in to add price lists {% endif %}

First, find a recent price list on your computer that you want to submit

{{ upload_example_info }}

Next, log in to CALC

To log in, click the login button at the bottom of any page on this website. You'll be taken to Cloud.gov and asked to enter your GSA login credentials. You should then be automatically redirected to page one of the "Add price data" form.

Then, upload your price list

Once you're logged in and viewing page one of "Add price data," you can upload your approved price list. You will be asked for very basic details around the contract (e.g. contract number, schedule name, business size, etc.). When you have filled that out, simply upload your contract to CALC.

You're done! It's that easy.

You should receive an e-mail when your price list has been approved. If you have any questions or suggestions, please reach out to us at calc@gsa.gov.
